Here is a Timeline of The Artist's life:

  • 1958 - Born in Minneapolis, Minnesota
  • 1960 - Younger sister Tyka born
  • 1973 - Meets Jimmy Jam and impresses with his work ethic and musical talent
  • 1975 - Pepe Willie forms the band 94 East, hiring Andre Cymone and Prince to record tracks
  • 1976 - Prince creates a demo tape with producer Chris Moon and is signed by Owen Husney
  • 1979 - Prince releases the album Prince
  • 1982 - Prince releases the double album 1999
  • 1984 - At one point Prince simultaneously held the No.1 album, single, and film in the US.
  • 1987 - Sign "O" the Times is released
  • 1989 - Prince is approached by Tim Burton to record the Batman nine-track album
  • 1990 - Prince goes back on tour for his back-to-basics Nude Tour.
  • 1993 - In rebellion against Warner Bros., Prince changes his name to
  • 1996 - Prince is released from his contract with Warner Bros. and releases Emancipation, the 36-song, 3-CD set.
  • 2004 - Prince is inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame
  • 2006 - Prince received a Webby Lifetime Achievement Award in rocognition of his use of the intenet to connect with his audience.
  • 2016 - Died in Chanhassen, Minnesota
